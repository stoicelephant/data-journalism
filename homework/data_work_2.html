Fun or Just Loud?

Today, we investigated your dog’s least favorite holiday: July 4th. By querying data from the 311 Service Requests Database and analyzing noise complaint frequency from 8 p.m. to 3 a.m., we were able to track down which NYC borough was having more fun than you...and made sure you knew it.

While it seems that all the Boroughs enjoy their fair share of parties, one stood far above the rest. It may not come as a suprise to hear that the Bronx loves to celebrate, but compared to the next runner up, they more than doubled that of Brooklyn. Going into this I expected the noise complaint pattern to be somewhat correlated with the size of the respective boroughs. To my suprise, Queens, the largest borough, by a pretty significant measure I might add, had the 2nd lowest number of noise complaints. The trends we found in the data suggest that size, or even population, were not nearly as significant as the individual culture of the borough.
